# Obsessia Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

## Privacy Policy

Obsessia (“the App”) is developed by Kruni Apps.

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected when using Obsessia.

---

# Information We Collect

## Account Information

If you create an account, the App may collect:

- Email address
- Authentication information provided through Firebase Authentication

## App Usage Data

The App may store:

- Tracker entries
- Session history
- AI conversation history
- Premium status
- Basic app preferences

This information is used to provide app functionality.

## AI Features

Messages sent to the Order AI system may be processed through OpenAI services in order to generate responses.

Users should avoid sharing highly sensitive personal information.

---

# How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

- Provide app functionality
- Maintain user sessions
- Improve app stability and performance
- Enable AI responses
- Support premium functionality
- Prevent abuse or misuse

---

# Data Sharing

Obsessia does not sell personal information.

Some data may be processed by third-party services required for app functionality, including:

- Firebase Authentication
- Google Play Services
- OpenAI API

---

# Data Security

Reasonable efforts are made to protect stored information.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

---

# Children’s Privacy

Obsessia is not directed toward children under 13.

---

# User Control

Users may:

- Delete tracker data inside the app
- Delete sessions inside the app
- Request account deletion by contacting support
